Company Profile

Shanghai Robust Electric was established in 2008, and serve as a manufacturer base for Aurora Motors – a well-known American electric motor company

Current production line can support up to 400 motors a month Can support added resources to produce up to 800 motors a month, or add production capabilities to develop /manufacture other non-traditional electrical or mechanical products
Development
  • 2008年: Shanghai Robust Electric was established
  • 2009年: Shanghai Robust Electric becomes a supplier of Horizontal Motors
  • 2010年: Shanghai Robust Electric becomes a supplier of Shaft Mount Reducer
  • 2011年: Shanghai Robust Electric becomes a supplier of Vertical Hollow/Solid Shaft
  • 2014年: Launched 3-year research and development program to develop next generation VHS product line
  • 2017年: Received UL listing for NEMA Premium Efficiency Motors
  • 2018年: Launched T-Frame motors, JM/JP, Permanent Magnet motors and ETO services
  • 2019年: Launch Bare Core Packages, smart motor technology, 50 hz motors, IP licensing program
